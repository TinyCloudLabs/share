How it works.

You choose exactly what can be read and who can read it. TinyCloud carries those facts from the sender to the recipient without turning the link itself into permission.

01

Choose

Select one Markdown resource, the exact recipient email, and when access expires.

02

Send

TinyCloud encrypts the invitation and gives you one link. The sender can go offline.

03

Verify

The recipient proves the matching email, then receives read-only access to the resource.

the trust boundary

The link is an invitation. Proof opens it.

If the email does not match—or the invitation has expired—the resource stays closed. Possessing the link is never enough for an addressed share.
